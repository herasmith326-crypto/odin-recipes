ADOBO

Adobo is a Filipino dish made by marinating meat in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. It is then simmered until tender and flavorful.

INGRIDIENTS

  • Chicken or pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns
  • Water
  • Cooking oil
  • Chilies
  • Sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Marinate the chicken or pork in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Put the meat and marinade in a pot, then add bay leaves and water.
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer until the meat becomes tender.
  4. Heat oil in another pan, then lightly fry the meat until browned.
  5. Return the meat to the sauce and simmer again until the sauce thickens.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning, then serve hot with rice.
