CALDERETA

Caldereta is a popular Filipino stew made with tender beef, simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce with vegetables, liver spread, and spices. It’s hearty, flavorful, and often served during celebrations

INGRIDIENTS

  • Baboy, beef
  • tomato sauce
  • liver spread
  • Garlic
  • onnion
  • Black peppercorns
  • potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • bell pepper
  • green peas

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. sauté aromatics – Heat oil and sauté chopped onions and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Brown the meat – Add beef chunks and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings – Pour in tomato sauce, liver spread, salt, and pepper. Simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add vegetables – Add potatoes, carrots, and other desired vegetables. Cover and cook until beef is tender.
  5. Add bell peppers and peas – Stir in bell peppers and peas, cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Serve – Adjust seasoning if needed, then serve hot with steamed rice.
